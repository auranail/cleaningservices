About

A simple way to connect customers and cleaners.

Let's Cleaning is designed to help customers submit cleaning requests and help cleaners register for new lead opportunities.

For customers

Submit your cleaning needs, location, and preferred timing so the request can be reviewed and quoted.

For cleaners

Apply with your service area, experience, and availability to join the cleaning network.

Simple process

Start with a quote request or cleaner application, then follow up with the details needed to move forward.
